Numerous LACITO members participate in the national and international research programs directed by Lacito members (see here in English and in French).

The LACITO currently hosts one of the two branches of the CRDO (Centre de ressources pour la description de l’oral – Resource center for the description of oral texts). The other branch is located at the Laboratoire Parole et Langage (LPL) (Speech and Language Laboratory) in Aix-en-Provence. The CRDO is a vast repository of oral archives, where the LACITO Archives may also be found (here).
