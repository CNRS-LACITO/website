Hafret Yi Kēye?
Mehabad, Iran, 2011, Ergin Öpengin

'Who deserves the woman?' lit. Whose is the woman?: A story with a religious content. A carpenter, a tailor and a priest, conjointly turn a tree trunk into a beautiful woman. Whose should be the woman? This question is addressed to the audience at the end of the story. It is told in third person's point of view, with little dialogue.
