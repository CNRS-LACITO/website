Bac and Dangem
New Caledonia, Kavatch [Kaavac], 1973, Rémi Pagu Faale, Françoise Ozanne-Rivierre

This text by M. Rémi Pagu Faale (Kavatch, 1973) is an observation of the habits of two freshwater gobies: Bac (Eleotris melanosoma) a small broadhead sleeper living in the depths, and Dangem (Awaous guamensis) who prefers to hide under rocks.
