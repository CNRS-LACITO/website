International Symposium

Information Grammar and Typology

25-27 March 2010, University Paris III, Maison de la Recherche,
4-6 rue des Irlandais, 75005 Paris

The Symposium is supported by the
Program « Information Structuring and Typologie »
Federation for Linguistic Typology and Universals (FR 2559, CNRS)

Contact : M.M.Jocelyne Fernandez-Vest

  • 15:00 Diana FORKER (Max Planck, EVA, Leipzig)

    [=Correlative and pseudo-cleft : the case of ‘de-Adjectif’ constructions]

  • 16:25 Danh-Thành DO-HURINVILLE (INALCO & CNRS-MODYCO)
    A pragmatic and syntactic study of the Vietnamese polyfunctional marker thi

Chair: Injoo Choi-Jonin
